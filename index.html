Corvid 19

It is a new world we live in. It has become clear that the us and states had advanced warning, but droped the ball. Was this attitude justified? How are you treating the pandemic? Looking back on what we now know, what changes can be made to better inform and motivate the public of a strong possibility?

      
Normalize

What will returning to normal look and feel like? So many are out of work. From hotel and market to resturant staff. How will the new hire surge be different then the past? How are we gearing up to help out new bussiness and entrepreneurs?

      
Bail Out

The house ways and means committee passed their budget for 2020/2021 with a 24-0 vote. This included an $128 million tax refund and an $800 million reserve fund for recession or natural disaster. Does our current corvid 19 situation qualify? If so, what is the best way to put it to work?
